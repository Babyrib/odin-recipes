Gari Mix

Description

A chilled cassava juice that is very common among Ghanaians

Ingredients

  • Gari (made from cassava)
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Frosty water
  • Groundnut

Steps

  1. Pour gari in your cup or mini bowl
  2. Add sugar
  3. Add groundnut
  4. Add water
  5. Add Milk
  6. Stir
  7. Enjoy your chilled Gari juice
