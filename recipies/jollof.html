Jollof Rice

Description

Rice stewed in a tomato and meat sauce,
a delicious savory West African dish

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne powder
  • 3 large tomatoes, diced
  • Â¾ cup water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into bite-size chunks
  • 4 cups jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil
  2. Pour diced onions in oil and wait for two minutes
  3. Fry you beef stew meat
  4. Add tomato paste and stir for 10 minutes
  5. Add minced ginger ,cayenne powder and minced garlic
  6. Stir and allow it to cook for 10 mins
  7. Add water (make sure the cups of water equalizes the
    cups of rice you would cook
  8. Add salt
  9. Allow it to boil hotter
  10. Add rice and stir till the rice and water completely mixes
  11. Reduce the heat
  12. Cover and allow it to cook
